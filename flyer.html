Contribute to Research with Neurodivergent Faculty and Generative AI Tools

Do you identify as neurodivergent? Have you tried generative AI (GenAI) tools (e.g., ChatGPT)?

Researchers at UMSI are inviting you to take part in a research study that seeks to understand and support neurodivergent (ND) faculty in their professional roles. Research shows that these tools have significant potential to support ND individuals by streamlining workflows and reducing cognitive load. However, little is known about their impact on ND faculty. The survey is the first phase of this study that focuses on your experiences with GenAI tools.

Study Details: Funded by the Office of the Provost's Disability Scholarship Initiative | IRB Approved (#HUM00274879)
